# Exp 1- Configure an IP Network

## What are the ARP packets and what are they used for?

### Quais são:
    Who has 172.16.120.1? Tell 172.16.120.254
    172.16.120.254 is at ec:75:0c:c2:3c:ac
    Who has 172.16.120.254? Tell 172.16.120.1
    172.16.120.1 is at ec:75:0c:c2:3c:75

### Para que servem:
    Estes pacotes servem para obter o endereço MAC associado a cada endereço IP
